Couscous

Description

Couscous is a North African dish made from tiny steamed balls of semolina flour. Though we think of it and cook it as a grain, it is actually a type of pasta.

Ingredients

  • olive oil
  • couscous
  • butter
  • salt

Steps

  1. To start, bring the cooking liquid (preferably a flavorful chicken or vegetable broth) to a boil in a medium pot.
  2. add the couscous
  3. Take the pan off the heat, cover, and let the couscous steam for 5 minutes.
  4. Use a fork to fluff it up and break up the clumps for light and fluffy couscous.
